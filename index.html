Admissions

We welcome students who demonstrate academic potential, strong character, and a desire to contribute to our thriving school community.

📅 Applications for 2025 are now open. Deadline: 31 October 2024. Early applications are encouraged as spaces are limited.

How to Apply

The Admission Process

1

Submit Your Application

Download and complete the Admission Application Form. Return with all required documents — previous school reports, birth certificate, and ID copies — to the school office or email admissions@excellencehigh.edu.za.

2

Entrance Assessment

Shortlisted applicants write entrance assessments in English, Mathematics, and Reasoning. This helps us understand each student's learning profile and place them appropriately.

3

Parent & Student Interview

Candidates and their parent or guardian attend a short, relaxed interview with our admissions team. This allows us to get to know your family and answer any questions you may have.

4

Offer & Enrolment

Successful candidates receive a formal offer of placement. Upon acceptance, families complete enrolment paperwork, pay the registration fee, and attend our welcome orientation.
